# MedPanel AI — Doctor Market Research (March 2026)

## Key Findings

### Swedish Market
- 1 in 4 Swedish physicians already use AI at work (Läkartidningen 2024)
- NO multi-specialist CDS tool exists in Sweden — gap is real
- Swedish MDT culture already values multi-specialist discussion (oncology-established, expanding)
- Remiss (referral) system bottleneck: 90-day guarantee never consistently met
- FASS (100% penetration), Janusmed (free, trusted), Internetmedicin.se (450K visits/week) are the current tools
- Cambio COSMIC becoming dominant EHR (Stockholm chose it 2025)
- Vitalis 2026 (May 4-7, Gothenburg) = #1 conference target
- Pricing: 395 SEK/month individual, $100K-500K/yr institutional
- Position as "case preparation tool" not "decision support" (avoids CE marking)

### Global Doctor Needs
- 66% of physicians use AI (AMA 2026), but 97% vet outputs — trust requires transparency
- Doctors 3.2x more likely to accept AI with literature citations
- 91.8% encountered AI hallucinations — multi-specialist cross-examination reduces this
- Referral wait times: median 79 days non-urgent, 36.4% get NO response
- Disagreement surfacing is MORE valuable than consensus in complex cases (Nature 2020)
- "Copy to clinical note" and "summary-first" are must-have UX patterns
- Peer endorsement is #1 adoption driver — one respected doctor > any ad spend

### Trust Builders
1. Transparency of reasoning (AMA policy 2025)
2. 3-second source verification (click → PubMed quote)
3. Named authoritative sources (NEJM, JAMA, Cochrane)
4. Appropriate uncertainty expression
5. Peer validation from respected colleagues

### Trust Destroyers
1. Hallucinations (84.7% say capable of causing harm)
2. Overconfidence without acknowledging uncertainty
3. Liability ambiguity (doctors bear liability, not AI makers)
4. Peer stigma (doctors using AI viewed as less competent)
5. Black-box reasoning

### Competitor Weaknesses MedPanel Exploits
- OpenEvidence: single-voice, no disagreement surfacing, pharma-ad funded
- UpToDate: $559/yr, dated UI, no patient-specific analysis, no cross-specialty
- Glass Health: single-model, documentation-focused, no multi-agent debate
- Isabel: DDx only, no case conference simulation

### Best Positioning
- Swedish: "Din digitala fallkonferens" (Your digital case conference)
- Global: "The only AI that disagrees with itself — so you don't miss what matters"
- Frame: "case preparation tool" (not decision support, not second opinion)
